NETHER COORDINATES CALCULATOR


The Nether is a dangerous dimension, but one of the greatest benefits it has to offer is its fast travel: travelling 1 block in the Nether moves you 8 blocks in the Overworld. That's because the Nether is 8x smaller than the Overworld. This calculator can help you find out where to go to in the Nether to reach a location in the Overworld. Just put in the coordinates, and it'll be converted from Nether to Overworld or the other way around.
