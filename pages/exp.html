EXPERIENCE & LEVEL CALCULATOR



Experience is an important stat gained by doing various things in Minecraft, from smelting ores to defeating mobs. Gaining experience increases your level, but the amount of experience to go to the next level increases after certain level brackets. If you need to figure out how much you need to do to have enough levels for an enchant, or just want to get to a certain level, this calculator is for you. Just put in what level you are and what level you need, and it'll do the rest.
