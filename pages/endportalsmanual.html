HOW TO USE: END PORTAL TRIANGULATION


  1. Turn on F3 mode.
  2. Throw an Eye of Ender. Travel in a straight line to where it lands. Do not face anywhere else.
  3. Input your current coordinates and angle in 1st Throw. (the angle is the left number in the "Facing: " part of the F3 menu)
  4. Using F3, turn 90 degrees left or right, then travel at least 100-300 blocks in that direction.
  5. Throw another Eye of Ender. Travel to where it lands.
  6. Input your current coordinates in 2nd Throw X and Z.
  7. Press [Calculate!]

Do know that the process can be somewhat inaccurate, so it is best to repeat this process until the Eye of Ender's landing location becomes consistent. Best of luck!



(N. R. G. Bodino, personal communication, December 16, 2025)
